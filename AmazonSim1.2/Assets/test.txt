Welcome to the Amazon drone simulator!
To get started, open the Menu in the bottom left corner.
Select a drone, then a destination, then click Dispatch.
You can move the camera by using the WASD keys or by moving your mouse to the edge of the screen in the direction you'd like to go.
You can also center the camera on a drone by clicking on the drone or by clicking on one of the small buttons on the status bar in the bottom left corner.
